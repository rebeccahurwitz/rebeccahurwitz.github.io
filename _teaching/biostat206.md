Course Syllabus
======
Professor: <a href="https://biostatistics.massgeneral.org/faculty/brian-healy-phd/">Dr. Brian Healy</a>
<br><br>
This course is reserved for participants in the Program in Clinical Effectiveness only. Introduces basic biostatistical techniques with an emphasis on applications to clinical research. Topics for the first half of the course include probability and statistics, hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, non-parametrics, and power calculations.
