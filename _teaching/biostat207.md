Course Syllabus
======
Professor: <a href="https://biostatistics.massgeneral.org/faculty/brian-healy-phd/">Dr. Brian Healy</a>
<br><br>
This course is reserved for participants in the Program in Clinical Effectiveness only. Introduces intermediate biostatistical techniques with an emphasis on applications to clinical research. Topics for the second half of the course include regression, paired and matched analyses, analysis of variance and multiple comparisons procedures.
